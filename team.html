研究團隊 / Research Team

AI Nudging for Sustainable Tourism Project Team

本研究由香港都會大學（HKMU）的學術團隊主導，致力於探索人工智能在推動可持續旅遊行為中的倫理與有效應用。 This research is led by an academic team at Hong Kong Metropolitan University (HKMU), dedicated to exploring ethical and effective applications of AI in promoting sustainable tourism behavior.
