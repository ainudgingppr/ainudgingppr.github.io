1. Dissemination Outputs, and Means

This table outlines all dissemination deliverables, target groups, distribution formats, and corresponding expected impact:

Output Type Target Format/Means Expected Impact

2.Expected Impact and Outcomes

Policy Impact

Provide evidence-based insights and ethical guidelines to inform Hong Kong’s sustainability and smart tourism policy agendas.

Industry Impact

Equip practitioners with concrete strategies and safeguards for deploying AI nudging in customer engagement.

Academic Impact

Contribute to interdisciplinary scholarship bridging tourism, AI, behavioral science, and public policy.

Public Awareness

Promote understanding of AI’s role in shaping sustainable behavior and stimulate public dialogue on digital ethics and environmental responsibility.
